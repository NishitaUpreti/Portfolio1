About Me!

I'm a freshman at Lovely Professional University with a strong interest in technology. I have experience in mobile app development, web design, and Arduino projects, and I'm skilled in Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I've worked on projects like a website promoting clean energy, an ultrasonic radar system using Arduino, and an e-booking platform for parking spots. Through hackathons and hands-on projects, I continue to sharpen my problem-solving skills and explore new technologies.

Projects

It is a user-friendly website that provides resources on clean energy solutions. It offers curated content to help users choose the right solar panel and explore affordable, sustainable energy options. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, the website is easy to navigate and informative.

This project showcased my skills in electronics, programming, and problem-solving. Technologies used include C++ and JavaScript.
